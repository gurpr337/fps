# Download More FPS! 🎮

A satirical web-based clicker game where you download FPS (Frames Per Second) and build your gaming empire through strategic upgrades and market manipulation.

## 🎯 Game Overview

**Download More FPS!** is an incremental clicker game that parodies the absurd concept of "downloading more FPS" for better gaming performance. Click your way to gaming supremacy by downloading FPS, selling them on the volatile market, and investing in powerful upgrades to automate your FPS empire.

## 🕹️ How to Play

1. **Download FPS**: Click the "More FPS!!!" button to instantly download FPS
2. **Sell for Profit**: Use "Sell FPS!" to convert your downloaded FPS into money (watch the market price!)
3. **Buy Upgrades**: Purchase upgrades to increase your clicking power and automate FPS generation
4. **Progress Through Stages**: Reach FPS milestones to unlock new stages with better upgrades
5. **Achieve Maximum Power**: Max out upgrades to earn achievements and dominate the leaderboards
